What is Feed the Hungry Nepal?

Feed the Hungry Nepal is a Non-Profit Organization founded on June 5, 2020, currently operating on a national level with over 100 active members and volunteers. We started as a youth initiative after considering the devastating impact Covid-19, and the subsequent lockdown had on the less economically able in Nepal.

How it Started

Niraj and Abhiyan were walking around Basantapur when they met Pooja and found out about a community that did not get any help during the covid lockdown. This is when they decided that the pandemic would not get the best of us.

Where it Took us

The first ration distribution at Kalimati where Niraj, Abhiyan, Shrinkhala, Nikki, Navashikha, Santosh, Ningwa, Aarya and Siza met Pooja again.

Where we are now

Feed the Hungry Nepal is now a legally registered Non-Profit Organization, working towards a hunger free Nepal. We distribute rations to those stranded by the pandemic and plan to take on the agriculture industry in Nepal by subtracting the mediator from the scene.
