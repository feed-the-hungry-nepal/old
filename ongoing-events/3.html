Ration Distribution.

18-07-2020, GODAVARI:

We went to Godavari again, after 6 days to help 55 more families with the help of Nepal Mahila Ekata Samaj.

12-07-2020, GODAVARI:

NMES(Nepal Mahila Ekata Samaj) found many families in need around Godavari. We were able to help 136 families on this particular day.

10-07-2020, KAUSHALTAR:

One of our supporters, Mr. Silwal helped us spot needy families in Kaushaltar again. We were then able to feed 50 families with his help.

10-07-2020, CHYASAL:

Nepal Mahila Ekata Samaj helped us find more families at Chyasal, Patan and so we went back to Chyasal to help 9 more families.

10-07-2020, DHOBIGHAT:

One of our members found and helped 2 families in Dhobighat.

05-07-2020, BALAJU:

Bhagwati Ma’am from NMES helped us locate and distribute 55 rations to a slum near Balaju Petrol Pump.

04-07-2020, MANDIKATAR:

Nepal Mahila Ekata Samaj (NMES) helped us find 73 families at Mandikatar, Budhanilkantha.

30-06-2020, CHYASAL:

Routine of Nepal Banda helped us find and help 2 families around Chyasal, Patan.

Feed the Hungry Nepal

Feed the Hungry Nepal is now a legally registered Non-Profit Organization, working towards a hunger free Nepal. We distribute rations to those stranded by the pandemic and plan to take on the agriculture industry in Nepal by subtracting the mediator from the scene.
