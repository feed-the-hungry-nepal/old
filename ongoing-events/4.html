Ration Distribution.

30-06-2020, KAPAN:

Bina didi, a member of an indigenous community in Kapan, reached out to us concerned about some families that were in dire need of food. We were immediately able to help 28 families there.

28-06-2020, THALI:

Despite the rain, we were able to distribute 50 ration kits at Thali, with the help of Mr. Kaji (founder of Heartbeat) and the Rotary Club.

24-06-2020, JORPATI:

Some of our members helped 11 families in Jorpati due to the help of a supporter of Feed the Hungry Nepal.

24-06-2020, KALOPUL:

One of the families at Kalopul reached out to us and Feed the Hungry Nepal was able to help 22 families.

22-06-2020, RANIBARI:

One of our core members helped a family in desperate need near Ranibari.

21-06-2020, KAUSHALTAR:

One of our supporters, Mr. Silwal found about 3 families that were in need near himself. Feed the Hungry was able to help them.

20-06-2020, DHAPASI:

Feed the Hungry Nepal connected with SHE organization. SHE organization helped us spot 25 families in Dhapasi, thus the 5th ration distribution event took place.

Feed the Hungry Nepal

Feed the Hungry Nepal is now a legally registered Non-Profit Organization, working towards a hunger free Nepal. We distribute rations to those stranded by the pandemic and plan to take on the agriculture industry in Nepal by subtracting the mediator from the scene.
