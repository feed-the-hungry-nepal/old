Ration Distribution.

16-06-2020:

Heartbeat is the first organization Feed the Hungry Nepal collaborated with since both initiatives aim to eradicate hunger. We helped them feed 14 more families.

11-06-2020, KALIMATI:

More friends and family joined the venture and helped 150 families at Kalimati, with the help of Pooja and the representatives of Ward no. 20.

05-06-2020, BASANTAPUR:

Two of our current core members noticed some people in need at Basantapur and helped them. This is when the idea of Feed the Hungry Nepal saw the light of day.

Feed the Hungry Nepal

Feed the Hungry Nepal is now a legally registered Non-Profit Organization, working towards a hunger free Nepal. We distribute rations to those stranded by the pandemic and plan to take on the agriculture industry in Nepal by subtracting the mediator from the scene.
