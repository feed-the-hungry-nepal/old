Ration Distribution.

13-08-2020, KAUSHALTAR:

We distributed 150 rations at Kaushaltar by coordinating with Nepal Mahila Ekata Samaj(NMES).

12-08-2020, Dhapasi

Dikshya ma’am reached out to us via social media and we distributed 32 ration kits in her orphanage.

11-08-2020, BALKHU HEIGHT:

We helped Binamra celebrate his 19th birthday by distributing rations to needy people he knew. He donated funds to help 55 families around Balkhu Height.

05-08-2020, DHAPASI:

Dr. Garima Shrestha, the founder of SHE Organization, helped us locate and feed 33 families at Dhapasi.

05-08-2020, BALAJU & MHEPI:

Two of our core members set out to help a total of 3 families at Balaju and Mhepi.

02-08-2020, ASAN:

We were contacted by some families in need in Asan. With the help of one of our supporters, we were able to help 4 families.

27-07-2020, GODAVARI:

2 more families were in need in Godavari from our previous distributions. Sowe went back to help them.

24-07-2020, KUPANDOLE:

We returned to Kupandole to help 28 other families in addition to the 76 families we helped on July 22.

Feed the Hungry Nepal

Feed the Hungry Nepal is now a legally registered Non-Profit Organization, working towards a hunger free Nepal. We distribute rations to those stranded by the pandemic and plan to take on the agriculture industry in Nepal by subtracting the mediator from the scene.
